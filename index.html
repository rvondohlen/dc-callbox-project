What is a Callbox?

Before telephones, callboxes were used by police and fire personnel as well as by the public, to communicate with the various centers of emergency communications. They come in various shapes which were representative of their function.

Why map them?

After a bit of research it seems that no one has a compete survey of the callboxes that currently remain in the district. Though historical maps exist and there have been localized efforts to map restoration projects, this is an effort to bring those together while allowing anyone to learn about these historical artifacts and contribute to their preservation.

How can you get involved?

If you have any corrections, questions, or additional information about call boxes, don't hesitate to reach out .Though the kinks are still being worked out, open contributions to the map are coming soon.
