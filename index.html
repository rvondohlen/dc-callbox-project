The District Callbox Project

Introduction

What is a Callbox Anyway?

Before telephones, callboxes were used by police and fire personnel as well as by the public, to communicate with the various centers of emergency communications. They come in various shapes which were representative of their functioned like to help make that happen sooner.

Behind This Project

Why map them?

After a bit of research it seems that no one has a compete survey of the callboxes that currently remain in the district. Though historical maps exist and there have been localized efforts to map restoration projects, this is an effort to bring those together while allowing anyone to learn about these historical artifacts and contribute to their preservation.

If you have any corrections, questions, or additional information about call boxes, don't hesitate to reach out. Though the kinks are still being worked out, open contributions to the map are coming soon. Reach out at the address above if you are interested in early access or would like to help make that happen sooner.
